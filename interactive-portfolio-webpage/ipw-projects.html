Console CRUD ×

Tired of overflowing grocery bags and surprise checkout bills? This handy shopping list program is here to be your digital shopping buddy!

  • Create lists with up to 100 items.
  • Name each item, specify the quantity you need, and enter the price.
  • Review your list anytime, displaying item details and calculating the total cost.
  • Update existing items on your list, modify quantities, or change prices.
  • Delete unwanted items from your list or clear the entire list for a fresh start.

So ditch the pen and paper (or the endless grocery store memos) and let this program be your shopping sidekick!
